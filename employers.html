Our Services

Choosing who to hire is one of the most difficult decisions that a hiring manager has to make. With the rising popularity of noisy digital recruiting solutions such as AI, Indeed, Monster and Linkedin, finding the hire that will transform your organization has never been more difficult.

Whether you are a large organziation looking to make one critical hire or you are an early stage startup looking to build a scalable team, we have solutions for every situation.

RETAINED SEARCH

When working on a retained model, we collect a partial fee before the recruitment process begins. The remainder is paid upon successful placement of the candidate. We will work on a 100% confidential and exclusive basis to fill your role

Retained search typically yields higher quality and more exclusive candidates, faster placement time and a closer partnership to our clients.

We offer a 90 day guarantee on all retained agreements.

EXECUTIVE SEARCH

Executive search seeks out the perfect match for both the role and the company - whether the candidate is actively looking for a new position or not.

Due to the confidentiality, exclusivity and specific skills needed, Executive Search is typically on a Retained model.

CONTINGENCY SEARCH

When working on a Contingency model, there is no fee paid to us until successful placement of a candidate.

We offer a 30 day guarantee on all contingency agreements.
